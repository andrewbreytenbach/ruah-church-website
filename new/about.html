We are a Church plant in the northern Indianapolis area. We are out of Exit Church in Gas City. Our desire is that all people will savor God and His Word.

We believe in the authority of Scripture.

The Bible is the inerrant and infallible word of God, his personal revelation to us, and the ultimate source of authority. It contains all things necessary for life and godliness.

We exist to make disciples of Jesus for the glory of God.

Theology and Evangelism are both components of spiritual maturity.
